Friday

On Friday I was set up in the call centre on the top floor, and shown what the customer service is like and how they deal with complaints, and then shown how to deal with the social media side of the complaints. I was then shown into the world of project management, where I met Eric and Matt, who showed me the ropes and told me what they both did. They are both in project management, but for different parts of the country. Eric was for Birmingham and Matt did mainly Swindon, but also did more projects around the country.
